How to Play

The mechanics of the game are very simple!

The game will start once you click the start button.

Dawg will move and you simply have to press the up arrow key in order to dodge the obstacles.

If you need to crouch in order to dodge obstacles in the air, press the down arrow key.

Do your best to avoid the obstacles in order to score higher points.

Every obstacle that you dodge adds to your score.

Once you have dodged 100 obstacles, you win! Dawg's adventure shall be over.

Goodluck and enjoy, and don't forget to recommend this game to your friends!
